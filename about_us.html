Goals and Mission

Our website was created to connect the sports-loving community and kind-hearted people, to join hands to contribute through volunteer running activities. Our goal is to raise funds and spread love to difficult situations in society.

Our mission is to organize regular charity runs, providing a healthy playground for everyone, while creating conditions for the community to join hands to help the less fortunate. Each step is a meaningful action that contributes to building a more compassionate and connected society.
